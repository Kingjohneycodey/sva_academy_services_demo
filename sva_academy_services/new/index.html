Designli is your digital partner.

Bring on a team of seasoned experts to define, design, build, and maintain your app or custom website. Designli works with clients from around the world who are building great businesses through technology initiatives.

UX and development services for web apps and mobile apps.

Sometimes founders, IT leaders, and stakeholders need help translating the vision into a polished, market-ready product. That’s where we come in.

Our cross-discipline team uses a clear process and vision to bring everything together. Our driving focus is setting clear expectations, providing top-notch communication, and delivering best-in-class products to our customers on time and on budget. With that, we hope to earn the privilege of becoming a vital part of your business as a long-term partner.

This approach is what took us from being the top-rated software firm in the Southeast and made us a national leader in the United States. Most importantly, it’s what allows our clients to plan the path to success and achieve better results, faster.

For mobile app projects, we're equipped with a team of cross-platform developers specializing in Google's "Flutter" technology. This allows us to build powerful systems that can run on both iPhone and Android devices. Flutter can even be extended to the web, drastically reducing software development costs (let alone maintenance costs) compared to previous norms.

When building web apps, we excel at the conceptualization, design, and custom feature development for complex web systems like those seen in our portfolio. We utilize Angular, React, and Node.js as our languages of choice, along with infrastructure built on industry-leading Amazon Web Services. We also work with numerous 3rd party frameworks to suit your business whether that’s mapping, analytics toolkits, or big data needs.
